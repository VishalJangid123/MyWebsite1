Welcome

Welcome to the web portal of the student branch of Computer Society of India - B K Birla Institute of Engineering and Technology. BKBIET became an Educational Institute Member of Computer Society of India (CSI) in June, 2016 with the intent to add its students from various disciplines to the field of mainstream Information Technology and Computer Science.

Computer Society of India is the first and largest society of Computer enthusiasts and IT professionals in India. CSI currently has more than 500 student branches and is rooted firmly at 73 different locations.

Membership drive for the student branch is currently ongoing. Membership forms are available with the Executive Committee members. To register online, see below.

        Why Join CSI-BKBIET

    We aim at imparting and gaining knowledge with the support of the faculty and CSI professionals. We plan to conduct workshops, seminars and talks by eminent members of CSI on technical and non-technical topics. With the support of CSI Jaipur Chapter and CSI Special Interest Groups, BKBIET CSI Student Branch contributes to the development of students in several ways providing them with a chance to meet and interact with other individuals at national & International level besides rendering a platform to showcase and recognize young talent in the field of Information Technology.
